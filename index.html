Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save with solar panels?

Most homeowners save between 50-70% on their electricity bills after installing solar panels. The exact savings depend on your energy consumption, system size, and local electricity rates.

What is the installation process?

Our process includes consultation, system design, permitting, installation, and activation. The entire process typically takes 4-8 weeks from start to finish.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer various financing options including solar loans, leases, and power purchase agreements (PPAs) to make solar accessible for all budgets.

How long do solar panels last?

Most solar panels come with 25-year performance warranties and can continue producing electricity for 30+ years with proper maintenance.
